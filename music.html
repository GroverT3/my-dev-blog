Bass and Trebled Waters

CALICROCODILE is an emerging artist from LA County whose music blends elements of Hip-Hop, Soul, Bass House, and Eexperimental Bass. His sound is characterized by atmospheric instrumentals, introspective lyrics, and a unique fusion of electronic and organic melty textures. CALI's work often explores themes of identity, personal growth, and the complexities of modern life.
